Sleeping Alone Data


What is This Dataset?

The information collected into this database comes from a survey conducted by FiveThirtyEight seeking to awnser how many people sleep alone and their reasons for doing so. You can see above in the pie chart the demographics of those involved in this study. Our website will allow you to explore these by gender, location, and relationship status. Additionally, you will be able to view data on individual cases within the study if you so wish. Below are some of the questions asked in the survey. Along with the possible responses.

Survey Questions:

Which of the Following Best Describes your Relationship?

Single; Domestic Partnership; Married; Divorced; Separated; Widowed

Do you Sleep in Seperate Beds?

N - Never; Y - Once a Year or Less; M - Once a Month or Less; MM - A Few Times a Month; W - A Few Times Per Week; D - Every Day; NA - Non Applicable; NO - No Awnser

Reason for Sleeping Separately? - Binary Responses

Snoring; Bathroom Use; Sickness; Initimacy; Arguments;

Does you Believe This is Helpful in Sleeping Better

0 - Strongly Disagree; 1 - Slightly Disagree; 2 - Neither; 3 - Somewhat Agree; 4 - Strongly Agree; 5 - No Awnser

Level of Education?

HS - High School; LT - Less Than High School; AD - Some College or Associates; BA - Bachelors Degree; GD - Graduates Degree; OT - Other

Income Level?

0 - 0-24,999; 1 - 25,000-49,999; 2 - 50,000-99,999; 3 - 100,000-149,000; 4 - 150,000+, 5 - Prefer Not Say
